Smoking Data Visualizations


Smoking is a major public health concern worldwide. This collection of visualizations provides insights into smoking trends, including the global prevalence of smoking, the relationship between cigarette prices and smoking rates, and the percentage of smokers by country.


Global Smoking Rates in 2022

The map above shows the smoking rates across different countries in 2022. The colors represent the prevalence of smoking, with darker shades indicating higher rates. Use the filters to explore the data and gain insights into global smoking trends.


Bubble Plot: Smoking Rate vs. Cigarette Price

This bubble plot illustrates the relationship between smoking rates and the price of the most sold brand of cigarettes in various regions. Larger bubbles indicate higher smoking prevalence.


Bar Chart: Percentage of Total Smokers by Country

The bar chart displays the percentage of smokers across different countries. It provides a clear comparison of smoking prevalence by region, showing which countries have the highest smoking rates.
